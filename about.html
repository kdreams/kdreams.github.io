About

The Pine Canyon Music Collective is a collaborative project based in Eugene, Oregon with the goal of creating a platform for independent artists to release regular music video content. Run and organized entirely by student musicians, our group enables our memembers to produce high-quality weekly releases featuring a variety of different creators of a variety of different styles.

Similarly to a record label, we provide our artists with both the personnel and resources necessary to produce their material. However — unlike a record label — the Pine Canyon Music Collective is created by artists, for artists. Rather than a money-driven pyramid system releasing content under the discretion of a producer, we create a unified support system for each other as artists, allowing us to reach new heights with our creativity.
